Titleist is the name most golfers reach for first — and our second-hand Titleist golf balls let you play it without the new-ball price tag. Sourced locally from courses across the West Midlands and Bristol, every ball is hand-checked, cleaned and graded before it goes in a box. Whether you're after Mint-grade balls that look barely played or budget-friendly Grade B balls for the range, Budget Birdies has a used Titleist option to suit your game and your wallet.

Popular models are known for their soft feel and greenside control — buying them second-hand from Budget Birdies means the same performance, hand-graded for quality, at a fraction of the retail price.
