Our story

Rescuing golf balls, giving them a new life.

Budget Birdies collects used golf balls of all brands and conditions from across the West Midlands and Bristol. We're committed to sustainability and availability — which means using eco-friendly methods to clean, package and ship our golf balls, while keeping them at unbeatable prices.

Every ball we rescue is one that doesn't need to be manufactured again, and one less piece of plastic left behind on the course. We're a small, local, hands-on operation — not a warehouse — and every single ball is checked by hand before it goes in a bundle.

What we care about

Sustainability

Rescued balls mean fewer new ones manufactured, and fewer left behind on the course for good. We clean, package and ship using eco-friendly methods throughout.

Availability

Quality, hand-graded balls at unbeatable prices — so a good round doesn't have to cost a fortune.

Genuinely local

Collected around the West Midlands and Bristol, by people who actually play on these courses.
