How it works

Simple, honest, and better for the course.

STEP 01

We find them

Balls collected locally around West Midlands and Bristol fairways, ponds and rough — instead of sitting there for good.

STEP 02

We check them

Every ball is washed, graded and hand-checked. Anything cracked, waterlogged or scuffed beyond play doesn't make the cut.

STEP 03

You play them

Bundled up and posted out with free UK shipping, at a fraction of the price of new.

Why buy rescued?

Every ball we sell again is one less new ball manufactured, and one less ball left behind in the rough. It's a small thing — but it adds up, one round at a time.
