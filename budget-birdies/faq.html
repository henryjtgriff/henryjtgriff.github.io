Frequently asked questions

How does the grading system work?
  • Mint: The A* of golf ball conditions. Looks and plays like a ball that has been hit merely once or twice.
  • A: Will play great, but with minor signs of wear such as small pen marks or very light scuffs.
  • B: More of a second-hand feel, but with no cuts or splits. May feature greater scuffs and discolouration. Perfect for the driving range or a casual weekend.

Our promise: anything cracked, waterlogged or scuffed beyond play does not make the cut.

How do we source our balls?

Our second-hand golf balls are sourced from around local courses, near lakes and in fields across the West Midlands and Bristol.

How do we prepare our balls?

Every ball is hand-checked, and individually cleaned and packaged into eco-friendly Kraft boxes.
